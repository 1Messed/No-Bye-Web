Don't just say Bye

Why You Shouldn't Just Say Bye

When ending a conversation, it's important to make the other person feel valued and appreciated. Instead of a simple "bye," consider saying:

  • "See you later."
  • "Take care."
  • "Goodbye for now."

More Tips for Better Goodbyes

  • Express gratitude: Thank the person for their time and conversation.
  • Offer well wishes: Wish them a great day, week, or whatever is appropriate.
  • Leave the door open: Indicate your willingness to connect again in the future.
